The Fellowship of the Ring is about of the a little hobbit who's uncle has the ring that is from and evil ruler who will destroy Middle Earth. So the ring has to be destroyed so the evil ruler called Sauron will not be in full strength to destroy Middel Earth. So a group of travelers have grouped together of all different races of dwarves, elves, hobbits and humans go across the world to go to Mount Doom to destroy the ring. Which they come across dangers and groups of evil horde of goblins and orcs to stop them.
